Alumni Career Summit

Campus Innovation Hub Hybrid Event Free Registration

Join us for the annual Alumni Career Summit, a day-long event designed to connect current students and recent graduates with successful TCU alumni across various industries. This year's summit features panel discussions, networking opportunities, and exclusive recruiter booths.

Event Highlights

  • Keynote address from industry leaders
  • Panel discussions on career growth and industry trends
  • Networking lounge with refreshments
  • Recruiter booths from top companies
  • Resume review sessions
  • Mock interview workshops

Who Should Attend

  • Current TCU students exploring career options
  • Recent graduates seeking job opportunities
  • Alumni looking to expand their professional network
  • Anyone interested in career development and mentorship

Registration

Registration is free but required. Please register by November 25, 2025 to secure your spot. Virtual attendees will receive a Zoom link 24 hours before the event.

For questions or special accommodations, please contact the Alumni Office at events@tcu.edu or call (555) 123-4567.
