Mentor applications for Spring 2026 cohort now open

We're excited to announce that applications for the Spring 2026 Mentorship Program are now open! This program pairs experienced alumni with graduating seniors to provide guidance, support, and real-world insights as they prepare to enter the workforce.

Program Overview

The mentorship program runs for 6 weeks, focusing on job search strategies, interview preparation, resume building, and professional networking. Mentors commit to weekly one-on-one sessions and provide ongoing support throughout the program.

What We're Looking For

  • Alumni with 3+ years of professional experience
  • Willingness to commit 2-3 hours per week for 6 weeks
  • Strong communication and mentoring skills
  • Passion for helping the next generation of TCU graduates

Program Timeline

  • Application Deadline: December 15, 2025
  • Mentor Selection: December 20, 2025
  • Orientation Session: January 5, 2026
  • Program Start: January 12, 2026
  • Program End: February 23, 2026

Benefits for Mentors

  • Make a meaningful impact on students' career journeys
  • Expand your professional network
  • Gain leadership and coaching experience
  • Recognition at the annual alumni awards ceremony
  • Access to exclusive mentor networking events

If you're interested in becoming a mentor, please complete the application form by clicking the "Apply" button below. We'll review applications and match mentors with mentees based on industry, career goals, and availability.

Questions? Contact the Alumni Office at alumni@tcu.edu or call (555) 123-4567.
